About Us

Little Things Café is a small and cozy café in Palawan.

We make Korean minimalist cakes, serve Korean meals, and offer delicious café drinks.

Our goal is to create simple but meaningful food moments for our customers.

We believe that even small things can bring joy, comfort, and sweetness to your day.

Visit us and enjoy the little things with us.
