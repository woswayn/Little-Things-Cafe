Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your store hours?

We are open from 3:00 PM to 8:00 PM (Monday–Tuesday, Thursday–Saturday).

2. Where are you located?

Pardeco Road, Rizal Extension Avenue, PPC, Palawan.

3. Do you accept advance cake orders?

Yes, we accept advance orders via email or phone.

4. Do you offer dine-in?

Yes, we offer both dine-in and take-out.

5. How can I contact you?

Email: littlethingscafepalawan@gmail.com or call 0968 854 5619.

6. Do you have Wi-Fi available for customers?

Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available inside the café.

7. Can I study or work inside the coffee shop?

Absolutely, you can study or work inside.

8. Do you accept cashless payments?

Yes, we accept GCash.

9. Do you have delivery available?

Yes, we deliver via Go Delivery Services.

10. Are pets allowed inside the café?

Pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area as long as they are leashed and well-behaved.
